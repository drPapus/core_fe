About Alghanim Travel

Alghanim Travel is a division of Alghanim Industries, the largest, most diversified privately owned group of companies in Kuwait. as the pioneer of the Kuwait travel industry, Alghanim Travel is the largest and oldest travel distributor in Kuwait, specializing in Business/Corporate travel, Holidays and Leisure, Retail outlets, Airline GSA's and a host of other travel related products and services.

Alghanim Travel represents British Airways, Gulf Air, Cathay Pacific, Qantas, Air Arabia and Eurorail. Alghanim Travel has a strong regional and global presence through its relationship with American Express network in the GCC, Middle East and world wide travel offices. The American Express Corporate Card provides a host of value add products and facilities and the ultimate travel management expense system. Alghanim Travel is firmly established as market leader and trendsetter in Kuwait's Travel Industry, spearheading innovative progress through adoption of modern management techniques and keeping abreast with International innovation in technology and online travel solutions.

Information Technology

is increasingly playing a major role in our business and driving the travel industry. Alghanim Travel is committed to International technological advancement and has implemented state of art travel solutions. The integration of our 'front office' reservation system with our 'mid office' and 'back office' allows us to provide a host of additional value added benefits and service enhancements, including:

  • Traveler profile synchronization, back office integration and end to end solution.
  • CRM – Travel policies and preferences, travel authorization, profiling etc.
  • Travel Policy Management with predetermined preferences and priorities.
  • On line portal for corporate traveler with built in controls, password and access levels.
  • Reservation status and travel information on line.
  • Customized access with use of on line facilities to suit client needs/policies.
  • Electronic vouchers for hotel and car reservations.
  • On line holidays and leisure products and services with payment gateway.
  • Customized and comprehensive management information reports.
  • E-Ticket tracker system, trip purpose codes and pre–trip/post–trip data.
